TECHINFO

In an effort to create employment opportunities for youths, the Benue State government collaborate with Troika Tex Digital Communications Ltd., BishBerry Digital Solutions Ltd., Google and Microsoft to conduct a comprehensive tech skill development for youth. The training is designed to equip the participants with digital skills and international certifications ranging from Digital Marketing and e-Commerce, Advance Data analytics, Data science and analysis, UI/UX (user interface/user experience) programming, frontend, backend programming CYbery Security and IT support to mention but a few. Because we are now heading towards a technology driven economy it is imperative for every Benue youth to be armed for the challenge ahead. It was in the light of the above, that this administration introduce the “Benue go digital skills programme” that would train no fewer than 2000 youths in various skills of their choice as stated above. The organization is dedicated to empowering youths with the skills and knowledg needed to succeed in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

At Benue Tech Skills, the team of experienced instructors and industry experts provide hands-on training and mentorship to participants, helping them develop practical skills and real-world experience. The programs are designed to be interactive, engaging, and challenging, with a focus on equipping participants with the tools and knowledge needed to tackle complex projects and solve problems.
